Where is my money stored?

Your capital is never transferred to us. It remains securely in your own MT5 (MetaTrader 5) trading account with your chosen broker.

Here's how it works:

  • Your Account: You maintain full ownership and control of your trading account at all times.
  • Our Products: Our MT5 Expert Advisors (EAs) are software tools that execute trades automatically based on our strategies.
  • Connection: You connect our products to your MT5 account, allowing them to place trades on your behalf.
  • Your Control: You can disconnect our products at any time, and you always have access to withdraw your funds directly from your broker.

We never have access to withdraw or transfer your money. Your funds stay with your regulated broker.

Can you take my money?

No, we cannot access your funds.

Our business model is based on providing trading software (Expert Advisors) that you install on your own MT5 platform. We do not have:

  • Access to your broker account credentials
  • Ability to withdraw funds from your account
  • Control over your capital
  • Permission to transfer money

You decide to use our MT5 products through your bank or broker. The relationship is between you and your financial institution. We simply provide the trading tools and strategies.

Your security is paramount. We operate transparently, and your money always remains under your control with your regulated broker.

What about taxes and regulations?

Tax obligations and regulatory compliance vary by country and individual circumstances. We recommend:

  • Consult a Tax Professional: Always speak with a qualified tax advisor or accountant in your jurisdiction about your trading profits and obligations.
  • Understand Local Regulations: Financial regulations differ by country. Ensure you're compliant with your local laws regarding trading and investments.
  • Keep Records: Maintain detailed records of all your trading activities for tax reporting purposes.
  • Broker Compliance: Choose a regulated broker that complies with your country's financial regulations.

Useful Resources:

  • Your country's tax authority website
  • Financial regulatory body in your jurisdiction
  • Licensed financial advisors
  • Your broker's compliance department

We are not tax or legal advisors. Always seek professional guidance for your specific situation.

What are the risks involved?

Important Risk Disclosure:

Trading in financial markets carries inherent risks. While we have a proven track record, you should understand:

  • Past Performance: Historical results do not guarantee future returns. Market conditions change, and past profitability doesn't ensure future success.
  • Capital Risk: You can lose some or all of your invested capital. Never invest money you cannot afford to lose.
  • Market Volatility: Financial markets can be unpredictable and volatile, leading to rapid gains or losses.
  • No Guarantees: No trading system or strategy can guarantee profits or eliminate risk entirely.

Our Commitment:

  • We maintain a transparent track record of our products' performance
  • We continuously work to improve our strategies and adapt to market conditions
  • Our reputation is on the line with every decision we make
  • We provide risk management tools and recommendations

We take our responsibility seriously. Every trading decision impacts our reputation, and we're committed to maintaining the trust our clients place in us.

Risk Management Tips:

  • Start with capital you can afford to risk
  • Diversify your investments
